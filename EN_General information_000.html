1. General information

Introduction

The User manual contains fundamental instructions that must be observed for the entire life cycle of the machine, and must be always be kept available at the place of use of the machine. The User manual is available in PDF format. Alternately, a paper copy of the document can be purchased from { [Manufacturer] }. Please contact your local supplier for details. In case of change of ownership, the User manual must be provided to the new user as an integral part of the machine.

Precautionary statements

Precautionary statements are reported as described below.

'Danger!’Warns of an imminent danger which will cause serious injury or death.

‘Warning!’Warns of a possible danger which could cause serious injury or death.

'Caution:’Warns of a potential danger which could cause moderate injury or damage.

'Note:’Provides information considered important but not related to dangers or risks.
